Built for images that travel

Give shared images a memory that survives the trip

TraceMark is made for ordinary sharing moments where origin, accountability, or proof still matters after the file leaves your device.

Memes That Keep Their Origin

Send social images, edits, and screenshots with an invisible identifier that can still be checked after compression and reposting.

Proof Before the Client Handoff

Photographers and visual creators can mark previews before delivery, then verify later whether a copy came from the original handoff.

Receipts for Rights Conversations

Keep a practical verification trail for copyright discussions, takedown requests, and provenance questions without adding visible branding.

Controlled Sharing, Clear Accountability

Share sensitive screenshots with distinct marks so redistributed copies can be traced back to the intended context.

Open Technology at the Core

TraceMark is driven by open source work and builds around Adobe TrustMark, bringing machine-readable provenance ideas into a practical mobile workflow.

Private by Design

Watermarking, detection, and matching run 100% on device. TraceMark does not upload your images or share data across the internet.
