TraceMark Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

TraceMark was built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. This app is designed to be a utility that functions entirely on your device. Data belongs to you, and TraceMark is designed to ensure it stays that way.

1. Information Collection and Use

TraceMark does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No Personal Data: TraceMark does not have access to your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Usage Data: It won't be tracked how long you use TraceMark, or what features you interact with.
  • No Image Data: While the app processes images to embed hex identifiers, these images are processed locally on your device. No images or image metadata are ever uploaded to a server or shared with any first, second or third party.

2. Permissions

To function correctly, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library / Storage: This is required solely to allow you to select images for processing and to save the modified images back to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Because the app does not require an internet connection and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs (like Firebase, Facebook, or AdMob), no data is shared with third parties.

4. Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your personal data being leaked from "servers" — because there aren't any involved. Your data's security is managed by your device’s operating system.

5. Children’s Privacy

No personally identifiable information from children is collected, as no information is collected from anyone at all.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Since we do not collect your contact info, we cannot notify you directly. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

TraceMark embeds machine-readable watermarks on device and detects them later when origin matters.
